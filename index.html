A group of cells having similar shape and function act together to perform a specific function in known as tissue.

There are two types of tissues in plant:

1.Mieristematic Tissue:
2.Permanent Tissue:

Meristematic Tissue

Meristematic tissues are those tissue which focus on the growth plants and have capacity to divide.These are enlongated,spherical,polygonal in shape and made up of thin cellulose cell wall.Its cells are living

.

Function of meristematic tissue:

  • This tissue take part in growth by formation of new cells.
  • The place of injury in plants is healed up the formation of new cells by meristems.

There are three types of meristematic tissue:Aphical Meristerm,Lateral Meristerm and Intercalary Meristerm.

Aphical Meristerm:These meristerm is found at tip of roots and stems,shoot apex and apices of leaves.The tissue help to grow shoots and roots
to grow longer in plants.This type of growth is known as primary growth.

Lateral Meristerm:These meristerm is found beneath the bark and vascular bundles of dicot leaves and stem.This tissue help to increase the
girth of plants.This type of growth is known as secondary growth.

Intercalary Meristerm:These meristerm found at base of leaves and stems.Such as stem of grasses,other monocots.It heps the leaves and other
parts to increase in length.

Permanent Tissue

Permanent tissue are composed of those cells which have lost their capacity to divide.This tissues have definite shape,size.Its cells may be living or dead.

Differentiation:The process by which the cells of meristematic tissue gives rise to permanent tissue is called differentiation.

There are two types of permanent tissue:

1.Simple Permanent tissue

2.Complex permanent tissue

Simple permanent tissue

Simple permanent tissue are those which are made up of only one types of cell.(eg:Parenchyma,Collenchyma etc..)

Simple Permanent tissue are of three types:

1.Parenchyma

2.Sclerenchyma

3.Collenchyma

Occurence:It is found in roots,stems, and cortex of carrot and also in mesophyll of leaves.

Fuctions:

This tissue supports plants.

Also transports the materias occurs through cell of parenchymsa cells.

This tissue acts as a storage tissue because it stores food and minerals in roots and stem.

There are two types of parenchyma tissue:

1.Aerenchyma:In hydrophytes (aquatic plants), large air are present to give buyancy.Such types of parencyma is called aerenchyma.(eg:Lotus,Lily.)

2.Chlorenchyma:If chloroplast is present in the parenchyma . Such parenchyma is called cholorenchyma.It performs photosyntesis.

Collenchyma

Sclerenchyma

Sclerenchyma:Sclerenchyma is made up of dead cells which are long,narrow in shape.It has thick cell wall.Intercelluar spaces are absent.

Complex permanent tissue

There are two types of Complex permanent tissue:

1.Xylem,2.Pholem

Xylem

Xylem is a conducting tissue.It is also known as wood and it is a mechanical and vascular tissue.Its cell walls are thick.

Xylem is made up of Tracheids,Vessels,Xylem Parenchyma,Xylem Sclerenchyma(Fibre).In which only xylem parenchyma is living and remaining three are dead.

Tracheids:Tracheids are enlongated in shape and made up of dead cells. Its main work to conduct water and minerals.

Vessels:Vessels are advanced and cylindriclal in shape,have thick walls and made up of dead cells.It main work is that to help the continous conduction of water in plants.

Xylem Parenchyma:These living part of xylem and its main work to store the food like starch in xylem.

Xylem Fibre:These are non living and have thick cell wall. Its main work to provide mechanical support to plant.

Function of Xylem:

a)It main work to conduct water and minerals.

b)The xylem parenchyma stores food.

c)It also provides mechanical support.

Pholem

PHolem is a conducting tissue.It is made up of sieve tubes,companion cells,xylem parechyma,xylem (sclerenchyma)fibre.

Sieve tubes:These are in tube like structure and help is conduction of food to other parts.Nucleus is absent.Sieve tubes posses slime protienwhich help in growth and repair of sieve tubes.

Companion cells:These cells help to sieve tubes to conduct water.Nucleus is present.Sieve tubes and companion cells originates from single mother cell so, they know as sister cells.

Pholem parenchyma:It main work to store food.

Pholem fibre(sclerenchyma):It provides mechanical support to sieve tubes.

Fuction of pholem:-

  • It helps in conduction of foods.

Protective Tissue

There are two types of protective tissue:

1.Epidermis
2.Cork

Epidermis

The outermost covering part of plant body is epidermis.It have thick cell.

It is found in the surface of leaves,roots,stem,flowers etc...

Fuctions:

  • It protect plants from injury,water loss and pathogens.
  • In desert plants, epidermis generates cuticle to prevent from loss of water.
  • It helps roots in absorbtion of water and nutrients.
  • Cuticle of epidermis also helps in preventing the entry of pathogens.

Cork

The outer protective coat of a tree is called cork.It is part of tree bark.

Fuctions of cork:-

  • Cell wall is thick due to presence of suberin
  • Protect the tree from fungal and bacterial infection.
  • It also protect from the loss of water.
  • It is light and don't catches fire easily.

Stomata

The tiny pores present on the surface of the leaves is called stomata.It main work to exchange the gases.

Epithelial Tissues

Nature: Epidermis is the outermost covering and protective tissue of plant.It xonsist of single cell and grow on some other types of tissues.

Occurence:It is found in inner lining of mouth,alveoli,kidney tubules etc...

Fuctions of Epidermal tissues

  • It protect the other organs by covering them.
  • Helps in absorbtion of water and exertion of waste.
  • Protect our cell from drying.

Types of Epithelial tissues:

1.Squamous epithelium:Its is flat,thin and irregular in shape to form linings.It is found in inner lining of mouth,oesophagus,alveoli(lungs) etc.It prevent our body from drying and mechinal injury.

2.Stratified squamous epithelium:When squamous epithelium is arranged in patter of layer,this epithelium is called stratified squamous epithelium.It is present in outer layer of skin and it also prevent from injury.

3.Cuboidal Epithelium:It is in cube like structure.It is present in kidney tubules,salivary gland,sweat gland,thyroid vessels.Its main function is to help in absorbtion and provide mechanical support.

4.Columnar Epithelium:This tissue is present in small instestine,alimentary canal,gall bladder and oviduct.It also helps in absorbtion and facilitates movement across epitheliar barrier.

5.Ciliated Epithelial:The hair like structure organ is called cilia.It is present in sperm duct,trachea,bronchi(lungs),nasal(cavaity).

Muscular Tissue

Voluntary muscles:Those muscles starts or stops according to our order.Those muscles are called voluntary muscles(eg:-striated muscle).

Inoluntary muscles:Those muscles don't starts or stops according to our order.Those muscles are called involuntary muscles(eg:-unstriated muscle).

Muscular tissu are elongated in shape.It has long fibre like cells calle muscles fibre. Muscles tissues are resposible for movement of our body and body organs like heart,alimentary canal.Muscles contains protein called contractile protein which helps in contract and relax to cause movement.

Types of muscles tissues:-

Occurence:These are mainly found beneath the skin,muscles,blood vessels,bone marrow .

Adipose Tissue:Nature:These tissue is made up of fat gobules or adipocytes.It is round and oval in shape and contains large drop of fats..

Fluid Connective Tissue

There are two types of fluid Connective tissue:

  1. Blood
  2. Lymph

Blood

Nature:It is a fluid connective tissue.It matrix is made up of plasma.

Occurence:It is found in arteries,vein and capillaries.

  • Blood platelets help in clotting of blood.

There are four types of our blood:

1.Plasma:Plasma is the colourless and fluid part of blood.It contains 55% of total blood volume and 90% of water and 7% of protein.It contains oxygen,hormones,RBC,WBC,Platelets .Plasma carry these from one part to another part of body.

2.RBC:Red blood corpuscles are also called erythrocytes.It is red in colour due to present of red pigment called haemoglobin.It transfer oxygen from lungs to body parts.

3.WBC:White blood cells are colourless and also known as leucocytes.WBC are present in less amount as compare to RBC.It is known as soldierr our body because it protect our body form various diseases and infections.

4.Platelets:These are also called thrombocytes and help in clotting of blood.

Nervous Tissue

Nerve impulses:The impulses that passes along the nerve fibre called nerve impulses.

Signals(Impulses):Impulses is the passage of electrical activity along the axon of nerve cell.

Nature:Nervous tissue contains highly speciliased cell called nerve cells or neuron.The functional,structural unit of our nerveous tissu is neurons or nerve.Nerve tissue is made up of neurons.

Occurence:It is found in spinal cord,brain etc...

Functions:-

  • It have ability to receive stimuli and conduct signals to different parts.
  • It transmits the message in our body.
  • The dendrites receive message and axon takes the impulses away from cell body and trasfer to brain.
Parts of Nervous Tissue:

1.Cyton:Cyton is called cell body covered by plasma membrance and contains nucleus and cytoplasm connects to dendrites.

2.Dendon & Dendrites:It is branched like structure that collect information from and converts in the form of signal and passes to cell body.

3.Axon:Axon is tube like structure that carries information from cell body to nerve ending.

4 Nerve EndingThe ending place of axon is called nerve ending.
